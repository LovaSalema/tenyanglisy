    Frequently Asked Questions about English Tests

    Which website gives a free English certificate?

    EF SET (efset.org) offers a free official English certificate after completing its 50-minute test. This certificate is recognized by thousands of employers worldwide and can be shared directly on your LinkedIn profile.

    How long does an English level test take?

    The Cambridge test takes about 25 minutes, the full EF SET test takes 50 minutes (or 15 minutes for the short version), and Test-English offers tests from 5 to 20 minutes depending on the target level.

    How to know your English level A1, B1 or C1?

    Take one of the 3 free tests recommended on this page. They assess your skills according to the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR) and give you a precise level from A1 (beginner) to C2 (mastery).

    Is the EF SET certificate recognized by employers?

    Yes, the EF SET certificate is recognized by more than 20,000 employers and academic institutions worldwide. It can be added directly to your LinkedIn profile.
